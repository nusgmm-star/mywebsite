Project Objective

This project aims to study the level of awareness about electronic waste and recycling practices among community members using a website, mobile application, multimedia content, chatbot, and online survey.

Why E-Waste Awareness?

  • Rapid growth of electronic devices
  • Environmental and health hazards
  • Need for responsible recycling
